Mario Kart Collector!

 
 

Instructions


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Mario Kart characters below. By clicking on a character, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number or you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each instrument is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Mario Kart character.
